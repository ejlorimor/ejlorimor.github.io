Here are a few things you can expect from a Financial Coaching Session.

Our coaches are volunteers who have completed our coach training and desire to assist God’s people in setting up a budget, saving, debt management, career planinng and learning how to honor the Lord with their finances.

Married couples should plan on attending together, otherwise very little can be accomplished.

  1. Coaching Process

    The coach will discuss biblical stewardship and will explain what God expects from His children. Our coaches are serving under the authority of Alliance Community Church.

    The coach will assist you in evaluating your present spending pattern and help you work towards a balanced spending plan.

    Once the spending plan is agreed upon, the coach will act as an accountability partner with you and will inform you of other studies that will enhance your understanding of biblical stewardship.

  2. Information Needed

    You may be asked to bring your checkbook register, income tax forms, paycheck stubs, bank statements, current bills, or other related information.

  3. Meeting Location

    You will, meet your Coach at Alliance Community Church where there are very few distractions and where there is sufficient table space to work on your budget.

  4. Session Length

    Your first session will run between 1½ and 2 hours. Subsequent sessions should run a little over an hour.

Please note: Our Coaches considers it an honor to serve the people of God. The Financial coaching referral service is a lay ministry of the volunteer coach, the local church, and Crown. There is no fee for this service. You may be given the option of purchasing material that will enhance your study of godly stewardship, but you are under no obligation to purchase any item or study. As mentioned above our coaches are volunteers who have completed our coach training and desire to assist God’s people in setting up a budget, Saving, debt management, career planinng and learning how to honor the Lord with their finances. Professional services such as investing, insurance, financial planning, and other related areas are not covered, other than to include these categories in a written spending plan if needed. If you are specifically looking for this type of help, we suggest that you contact a professional near you.
