Volunteer Job Board
Web Developer

Join the IT team and help update and maintain the website, training webinars, Facebook and SharePoint. Training is provided. The position requires 2 – 5 hours a week and attending monthly meetings.

Financial Coach

Coaches are volunteers who completed a Certified Coaching training program: The coaches discuss Biblical stewardship and explain practical applications, as well as assist in evaluating present spending pattern and help individuals work towards their goals and a balanced spending plan. Requires 3 – 5 or 5 -10 hours a week and attendance of monthly meetings.

Compass Referal Coach

Howard Dayton and Steve Moore Spend a half hour each weekday helping listeners with their financial questions. Money Wise offers a practical, biblical and good-natured approach to managing your time, talents and resources.

The show generated quite a bit of listener interest, which also resulted in calls and email to the ministry from folks looking for financial guidance and help. A Compass Referral Coach responds to these inquires. Requires answering 2 or more questions a week and attendance of monthly meetings.

Small Group Facilitator

Small Group Leaders facilitate Biblical studies, which provide practical applications of handling personal finances and shares God’s perspective of managing money and possessions. Training is provided and the role requires 3 – 5 hours a week and attendance of monthly meetings.

Online Small Group Facilitator

Facilitate the online nine week small group study, Navigating, which teaches individuals God’s perspective of money, possessions and more. The study includes practical applications of handling finances, and transform both life and the handling of money. Requires 2 – 3 hours a week and attendance of monthly meetings.
